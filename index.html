A brief history of Merida Yucatan

The city was founded the 6th of January of the year 1542 by Francisco de Montejo "El mozo". Before the Spanish arrived it was known by the mayans as "T'ho" which translates to "five hills".

What about the food?

For me food is one of the most important parts of any place, I love food from everywhere but theres a special place in my arteries for the food in Merida. There's no food here in Merida that doesn't have these two things: Oil and Tortillas. Personally I love the Relleno negro the Salbutes and of course the Tortas de Cochinita.
